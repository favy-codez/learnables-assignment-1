Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is an athletic, intelligent dog,it belongs to the Spitz genetic family. It is recognizable by its thickly furred double coat, erect triangular ears, and distinctive markings. It originated in Northeast Asia where they are bred by the Chukchi people of Siberia for sled pulling, and companionship.Today, the Siberian Husky is typically kept as a house pet, though they are still frequently used as sled dogs by competitive and recreational mushers.
